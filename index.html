The process

Simple by design,
meaningful by nature

Four steps to turn your possessions into a clear, compassionate legacy.

01

Photograph your items

Capture photos of meaningful belongings — jewellery, furniture, art, keepsakes. Add a note about the story behind each one.

02

Assign recipients

Choose who should receive each item. Add a personal message explaining why you want them to have it.

03

Recipients acknowledge

Loved ones receive a gentle notification and can acknowledge their assignment — creating mutual understanding while you're still here.

04

Generate your document

Export a clean, signed personal property memorandum — a legally persuasive companion to your will that reduces family conflict.
