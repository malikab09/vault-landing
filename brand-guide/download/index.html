JPG note. The wordmark and template JPG renders use Lora as a Fraunces stand-in, because Fraunces couldn't be embedded at the JPG render step. For brand-accurate raster output, open the SVG version in Figma, Illustrator, or Keynote (with Fraunces installed via Google Fonts) and export from there.
